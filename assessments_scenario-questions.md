## How to Self-Assess

1. **Start with domain questions** to identify weak areas. If you score below 70% on a domain, review the corresponding curriculum module before proceeding.
2. **Then do scenario questions** to practice applied reasoning across domains.
3. **Track which task statements you miss.** Use `docs_cross-reference-map.md` to find the relevant curriculum sections for review.
4. **Focus extra time on Domain 1** (27% weight) and domains that appear in the most scenarios (Domain 5 appears in 4 of 6).
